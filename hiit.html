MotoheadThe band was founded in 1975 by already experienced musicians. Their leader Ian “Lemmy” Kilmister was a former member of the space rock band Hawkwind. Larry Wallis had played with Pink Fairies. Eddie Clarke had been a member of Curtis Knight’s Zeus. Their previous experience is one element which divides critics and fans over whether the band belongs to the new wave of the British heavy metal.
